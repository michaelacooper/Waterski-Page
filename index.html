Waterskiing in Denver

Most people think about Colorado as a snowskiing hub, since many of the worlds top resorts are located within a 2-3 hour drive from the city. However, it is a suprise to many that there are 10+ lakes with slalom courses within an hour drive of Denver.

Not only are the slalom lakes plentiful, the season isn't short either. Yes, Denver does get a late spring and even sometimes a summer snow storm, but it also boast an average 300 days of sunshine each year. While weather is unpredictable, the hardcore waterskiers can typically ski from time change to time change (Mid-March to late October).
